MTJ Welding & Fabrication is a Salt Lake City steel shop run by a working firefighter — on duty when the call comes in, in the shop the rest of the time, building metalwork that holds up for decades.

Every handrail, gate, and weld leaves the shop the way I'd put it on my own home: square, clean, and built to last. No shortcuts, no excuses.

From custom residential railings to large-scale fabrication and roadside repairs, MTJ brings the same discipline you'd expect from the fire service to every job.
